Who They Are

South India's trusted IT & connectivity partner

INI Technologies Pvt. Ltd. is a Bengaluru-based managed IT services company specialising in enterprise connectivity, telecom reselling, and digital infrastructure delivery. Founded with a focus on bridging global telecom infrastructure with regional enterprises, INI has built a substantial presence across Kerala and Karnataka as an authorised Tata Communications partner.

The company's strength lies in localised sales and support — offering enterprises the reliability of Tata Communications' Tier-1 network with the personal service and competitive pricing of a regional specialist. From dedicated internet leased lines to CDN services, INI serves a cross-sector portfolio spanning IT parks, startups, gaming, e-commerce, BFSI, and education.

About the Company

The backbone of global enterprise connectivity

Tata Communications (formerly VSNL) is a global digital ecosystem enabler that powers the digital ambitions of businesses across industries. Operating one of the world's most interconnected subsea cable networks — spanning ~500,000 km — it provides the infrastructure backbone for a vast share of the world's internet traffic.

Listed on the NSE and BSE, Tata Communications serves Fortune 500 companies, governments, and fast-growing enterprises through a portfolio that spans network connectivity, cloud, cybersecurity, collaboration, and media services.

In India, Tata Communications operates as a Tier-1 ISP — the highest classification — meaning it maintains direct peering relationships globally, making it one of the fastest and most reliable backbone providers for Indian enterprises requiring international connectivity.

Project Objective

What the internship set out to achieve

INI Technologies required a structured, scalable B2B lead intelligence database to power their Tata Communications reselling operations. The internship was commissioned to build this from the ground up — spanning IT park directories, LinkedIn enrichment, Salesforce CRM, and Python automation across Kerala and Karnataka.

The overarching goal: transform raw IT park directories and fragmented web data into a clean, structured, Salesforce-ready prospect database — segmented by geography, vertical, company size, and decision-maker contact.

Every output from the internship was designed to be immediately actionable by the INI sales team — not academic deliverables, but live CRM inputs, enriched LinkedIn profiles, and segmented outreach lists.

Methodology

The 7-phase execution plan

A structured, sequential approach — each phase building on the last to deliver a complete, layered sales intelligence asset.

Phase 1
Data Discovery & Source Identification
Identified and evaluated primary data sources: IT park official directories (Infopark, Technopark), LinkedIn Sales Navigator, JustDial, company websites, Airtel/Tata partner portals, and funding databases (Crunchbase, Tracxn). Established master data schema, naming conventions, and sheet architecture for the 53-deliverable project structure.
Phase 2
Kerala IT Park Mapping
Systematic company-by-company mapping across all 6 Kerala regions: Kochi Infopark (4 phases · IPA, IPB, IPC, IPD · 433 companies), Trivandrum Technopark (3 phases · TPA, TPB, TPC · 520 companies), Calicut Cyberpark (72), Thrissur Infopark (49), Kollam Technopark (31), Cherthala Infopark (15). Consolidated into 1,457-company Kerala master database.
Phase 3
LinkedIn Sales Navigator Enrichment
Cross-referenced park data with LinkedIn to build company profiles, identify decision-makers (CTO, IT Manager, Head of Infrastructure, Network Engineer), and verify employee counts. Built 1,528-company Kochi LinkedIn database and Trivandrum LinkedIn mapping. Identified target roles across all verticals for outreach personalisation.
Phase 4
Bangalore Tech Cluster Mapping
Extended the mapping to four Bangalore clusters: Electronic City (257 companies, in progress), ITPB — International Tech Park Bangalore (77), Manyata Tech Park (149, completed), and Embassy Tech Village (36, in progress). Consolidated into a Bangalore master database with company names and initial profiling.
Phase 5
Vertical & Sector Segment Lists
Built specialised outreach lists by vertical: Gaming companies and providers (55), E-commerce websites (50), CDN providers from Kurian Sir's team (39), Indian Mobile Brands (12), Channel partners — India-wide IT reseller list (901), Funded companies FY2025 (1,067), AI-funded companies, Airtel partners, Tata Tele partners, JustDial partners, Antivirus companies, and Online stores.
Phase 6
Salesforce CRM Enrichment & SFDC Upload
Mapped and validated 897+ Salesforce SFDC accounts from Vineeth's existing pipeline. Cross-referenced each account with LinkedIn for contact accuracy and data completeness. Cleaned, standardised, and enriched records for direct CRM upload. Remaining balance accounts identified and flagged for team allocation.
Phase 7
Automation, AI Tools & Documentation
Developed Python automation projects for web scraping and contact data extraction. Built AI-powered meeting recording transcription and structured note generation system. Evaluated Apollo.io vs Lusha in a full feature, pricing, and ROI comparative report. Compiled GPT chat history and prompt logs as a reusable team knowledge base.
